Privacy Policy

App: Differently  ·  Developer: AutismResearch.ai  ·  Last updated: May 1, 2026

Differently is designed to support communication for autistic individuals. We take the privacy of our users — and especially the children who use this app — very seriously. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service and never sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Differently is developed and operated by AutismResearch.ai. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at contact@autismresearch.ai.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Differently:

  • Account information — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID.
  • Communication data — symbols selected, phrases built, and sentences spoken within the app. This is used to personalise AI suggestions.
  • Usage data — which features are used, frequency of use, and AI request counts. Used to improve the app and manage subscriptions.
  • Subscription data — payment status managed via Stripe. We do not store full card details; these are handled securely by Stripe.
  • Device information — device type and OS version, used for debugging and compatibility.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, or any information unrelated to the app's communication features.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Differently is intended to be used by parents, caregivers, and therapists on behalf of children. The app is not directed at children under 13 to use independently.

  • Accounts are created and managed by adults (parents, caregivers, or therapists).
  • Communication data associated with a child is stored under the adult's account.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us at contact@autismresearch.ai and we will delete it promptly.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the AAC communication features.
  • To power AI-enhanced suggestions via the Claude API (Anthropic).
  • To manage your subscription and process payments via Stripe.
  • To train and improve on-device inference models (using anonymised, aggregated data only).
  • To send important service communications (e.g. subscription renewal notices).

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following trusted third parties, strictly to operate the service:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage (Firebase Privacy Policy).
  • Anthropic — AI suggestions are processed via the Claude API (Anthropic Privacy Policy).
  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy).

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (US-based servers). We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase Security Rules to restrict data access, and server-side API key management (your Claude API key is never stored on-device).

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data remains accessible and exportable. You may request full deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing contact@autismresearch.ai.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (use the "Download my data" feature in the app).
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability — export your data in machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@autismresearch.ai.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact:
