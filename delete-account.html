What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Google account sign-in information
  • All communication data (symbols used, phrases, sentences)
  • AI usage history and monthly usage stats
  • Custom vocabulary and board configurations
  • Subscription records (Stripe billing info is deleted separately by Stripe)

What Is Retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated usage data used for app improvement (no personally identifiable information)
  • Financial transaction records retained for up to 7 years as required by law

⚠ This action is permanent. Deleted data cannot be recovered. We recommend using the "Download my data" feature in the app before requesting deletion.

Delete Only Your Data (Keep Account)

If you'd like to delete your communication data and usage history but keep your account active, mention this in your email and we will delete only the specified data within 7 business days.
